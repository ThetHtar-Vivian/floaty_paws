About Floaty Paws

Soar high, avoid obstacles, and have fun!

Game Overview

Floaty Paws is a playful Flappy Bird–style game. Tap or press space to keep the cat floating while avoiding obstacles and collecting stars!

Controls

Tap or press space to keep the cat in the air.

Obstacles

Pipes, floating objects, and wind gusts can knock you down!

Collectibles

Stars increase your score and unlock fun balloon skins.

Goal

Fly as far as you can and beat your high score!

Technologies

HTML5, CSS3, JavaScript, Canvas API, LocalStorage for scores.

Future Improvements

Mobile controls, new balloon types, power-ups, and online leaderboards.

Watch the Game in Action

Reflection

Developing smooth physics for the cat and balloon mechanics was challenging, but iterative testing helped achieve responsive controls and fun gameplay. Future versions will focus on polish, animations, and extra game modes.
