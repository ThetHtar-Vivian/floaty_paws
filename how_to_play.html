How To Play

Help your cat pop balloons and survive as long as possible!

Objective

  • Keep your cat flying using the balloon (gravity pulls you down)
  • Pass safely through obstacles (one hit = game over)
  • Survive as long as possible (longer time = higher score)

Controls

  • Tap / Click → Flap upward (short taps work best)
  • Release → Fall downward (don’t panic)
  • Timing is everything (find a rhythm)

Game Modes

Classic Mode

Endless play with increasing speed.

(for people who love challenges)
Challenge Mode

Timed levels with tricky obstacles.

(for people who are newbies)

Tips

  • Tap gently (spam clicking makes control harder)
  • Stay near the middle (gives you reaction time)
  • Focus on rhythm (not raw speed)
