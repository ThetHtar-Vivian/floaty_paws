Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2026

Overview

Floaty Paws is a browser-based game hosted on GitHub Pages. Your privacy is important, and this page explains what data (if any) is handled while playing the game.

Information We Collect

We do not collect personal information such as names, email addresses, or passwords.

The game may store limited data locally in your browser (such as selected characters or high scores) using localStorage. This data stays on your device and is never sent to a server.

Cookies & Tracking

Floaty Paws does not use cookies, analytics, advertisements, or third-party tracking tools.

Third-Party Services

This website is hosted on GitHub Pages. GitHub may collect basic technical information (such as IP addresses) according to their own privacy policy.

Future Features

If features such as online leaderboards or user accounts are added in the future, this privacy policy will be updated accordingly.

Contact

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact the developer.
